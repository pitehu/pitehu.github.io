Biography

I am a first-year second year third year PhD student (Gates Scholar, Apple Scholar) in Computation, Cognition and Language at the Language Technology Lab at University of Cambridge, supervised by Prof. Nigel Collier. I have broad interests in natural language processing and computational social science. Most recently, my research encompasses two aspects: 1) developing personalized language models and 2) leveraging language models to power social science simulations. I like mountains, lakes and the ocean.
