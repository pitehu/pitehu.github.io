Biography

Grüezi! Howdy, Y’all!

I am Tiancheng Hu, a 2nd-year Master’s student in Information Technology and Electrical Engineering at ETH Zürich, and a part-time research intern at DeepJudge.

I am an aspiring researcher in natural language processing and computational social science. In the broadest sense, I am interested in building and applying methods for social science problems.

Previously, I obtained a Bachlor’s of Science in Electrical Engineering from The University of Texas at Dallas, advised by Prof. Carlos Busso. I worked on driver head pose estimation with 3D data.

Here is my resumé.
